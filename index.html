I'm Andrew

About

I am currently a Senior studying Data Science with a GIS focus at UC Berkeley. I am also research assistant at the Girotto Lab, working under Manuela Girotto PhD.
At the Girotto Lab I am currently working on a project to process groundwater data in Northern Italy and process a time series analysis.
I am additionally working with a team of three undergraduate students to stream data into a Recurrent Neural Network model to predict predicipation levels in the Sierra Nevada mountain range, based on ocean temperatures.
